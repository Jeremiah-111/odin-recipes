Fajitas

Description

Chicken fajita meat is seasoned, thinly sliced chicken breast cooked in a skillet with a blend of chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and paprika. It's juicy, flavorful, and perfect for serving in warm tortillas with sautéed peppers, onions, and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast (sliced into thin strips)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 lime (optional for extra flavor)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Chicken:Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips.
  2. Season:In a bowl, mix the olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic poweder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken and toss until well coated.
  3. Cook:Heat a skillet or pan over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the chicken strips and cook for 7-10 minutes, stirring occasionally until fully cooked and golden brown.
  4. Optional:Squeeze fresh lime juice over the chicken for a zesty kick.
  5. Serve:Serve with tortillas, sautéed peppers, onions, or your favorite toppings.

Simple Beef Tacos

Description

Beef tacos are a quick and flavorful meal made with seasoned ground beef, served in soft or crunchy taco shells with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or 1 tbsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp paprika)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Taco shells (soft or hard)
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, lettuce, tomato, sour cream, salsa

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the ground beef until browned, breaking it apart as it cooks.
  2. Drain excess grease.
  3. Add taco seasoning and water, stirring the combime. Simmer for about 5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  4. Serve in taco shells and add desired toppings.

Speghetti

Description

A classic spaghetti dish with a rich tomato sauce and tender pasta, perfect for a comforting, quick dinner.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti noodles
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 lb ground beef or Itilian sausage (optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves(minced)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add salt, and cook the spaghetti noodles until al dente (about 8-10 minutes). Drain.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add ground beef (if using) and cook until browned. Drain excess grease.
  4. Pour in marinara sauce, stir, and simmer for 10 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the sauce. Serve with Parmesan cheese if desired.
